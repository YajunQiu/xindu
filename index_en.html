About

“Provide customers with quality coating products of international level and post-sale supporting services in compliance with the Chinese market.”

Shanghai Jiangchang Industry Co., Ltd. was incorporated at Shanghai Jinshan Chemical Park in 2010 and is a domestic enterprise specialized in chemical anti-corrosive paints. The Company has been engaged, for years, in sales and technical services in the paint industry and has formed a well-developed and perfect sale channels and post-sale system. Additionally, in cooperation with Wuhan Modern Technologies Institute with the background of Wuhan University, the Company possesses the R&D and production base for advanced functional paints and has a professional high-end R&D team of chemical anti-corrosive paints with rich technologies and experiences in paint manufacturing.

Since its establishment, the Company has ensured customers can, no matter where they are, obtain the high-quality, trustful and professional technical support and services in line with aim of providing customers with technical consultancy and quality products and resolving problems for customers.
Its products are sold to such regions as East China, South China, North China and Northwest China and are widely applied in such markets as municipal engineering, electric power, high-speed railway, expressway, hot-dip galvanizing repair and renovation, wind power and solar PV generation.

XINDU cold galvanizing KX

  • Dry film zinc content: 96%
  • Heavy-duty anti-corrosive material, with lasting dual-effect, composed of organic/ inorganic hybrid resin composite polymer and imported high-purity zinc

Usage of Cold Galvanizing

Repairing: 450ml aerosol preparation

  • Before using, clear thoroughly dust, oil stain, iron rust and old coating film from the surface of components to be sprayed, and ensure the surface is dry.
  • Before spraying, it is necessary to shake the tin up and down, left and right for about 2min till plaint liquid is evenly mixed before being coated directly on the steel or galvanized surface.
  • With a spraying space of 20-30cm, press down, with the forefinger, the nozzle and move horizontally in turns for coating at an event speed; to ensure the coating film thickness of 40μm in spraying, the spraying speed should be about 10cm/s. In coating, make sure to avoid spraying around or on human body.
  • If spraying is not completed in one go, keep the paint tin upside down before storing, press hold the nozzle for about 3s in a safe place, and clear the residual paint from the nozzle to prevent any choke when using again.
  • In case of coating on other undercoat or residual coating film, the product will not play a role of electrochemical protection and lose its anti-corrosion performance. Additionally, for pretreatment of surface, the thickness of coating film will also affect the anti-corrosion performance of the product.


Engineering: 25kg and 2.5kg package

  • Opening the barrel (tin) for inspection
    Check carefully and confirm whether the type, mark, color and effective storage period of the paint conforms to the requirement and then clear the dust away from the barrel cover and its surrounding area to avoid any foreign stuff from getting in.
    Upon opening the barrel (tin), in case of any skinning observed on the surface, cut the paint coat along the edge for taking out, without squashing at random and without mixing the crushed paint coat into the paint. In case of observing the paint with serious expanding and caking, it is necessary to have the technical inspector to check before determining whether to use it.
  • Coating process
    • Steel cutting—welding for assembly of parts—blasting for rust-removing—joint pre-coating with cold galvanizing paint—spraying with cold galvanizing paint—packing and shipping to the work site—welding for assembly—joint grinding—supplementary coating with cold galvanizing paint—inspection of coating quality-repair of damaged coating film.
    • Xindu cold galvanizing paint is applied with the high-pressure airless spraying method. Before being used, it must be adequately blended. In coating, it shall be blended once every 10-15min, by coating while blending. The spraying pressure is MPa10.0-12.0 and the nozzle diameter is 0.48-0.63mm. The coating temperature range for cold galvanizing is: -5~50° C (actual application at-20° C~75° C); the relative humidity: < 85° C.
    • Coating time interval: 50~90min (summer ~ winter); it will be ready when coating film surface is dry.
